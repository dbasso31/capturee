This summer, Capturée was invited by The festival Calvi On The Rocks. Everyday during the festival, as David was taking pictures, Jérôme was playing records on one of the most beautiful spots of Calvi.

And as you can expect, this was not your average party photo reporting, and not your average post-electro DJ set. Consider it more as a residency, where Capturée tried once again to "capture" something from Calvi a bit different than what you could expect.

This is how we lived Calvi this year and what we captured of it.

This is our Calvi.
