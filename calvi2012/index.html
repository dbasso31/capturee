This summer, Capturée was invited by the festival
Calvi On The Rocks.

Everyday during the festival, on one of the most beautiful spots of Calvi, David was taking pictures, and Jérôme was playing records.

But of course this was not your average party photo reporting, and not your average electro DJ set.

Think it as a residency, where Capturée tried once again to capture something from Calvi a bit different than what you could expect.

This is how we lived Calvi this year and what we captured of it.
